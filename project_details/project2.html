This project was my final project for my Computer Vision class. The aim of the project was to look at the problem of content aware image resizing to avoid bad aspect ratios we end up having when we resize images.
We looked at both classical and deep learing approach of the task. For the more traditional approach we looked at seam carving which is based on using seams and removing low energy pixels of the image. To overcome the issues (distorted objects) with seam carving we make use of a deep learing approach based of using saliency maps. Our deep learing approach is based of this paper
To conclude we suggest a hybrid approach of using both carving and saliency maps (followed with some morphoogical operations) to get the best results.
